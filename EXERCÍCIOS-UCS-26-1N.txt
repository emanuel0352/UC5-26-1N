Leitura
Maria is happy today. Yesterday she was tired. Tomorrow she will be relaxed.

Responda:

Is Maria happy today?
Was she tired yesterday?
Will she be relaxed tomorrow?
Escrita
Complete:

She ___ my friend.
They ___ at school.
I ___ tired yesterday.
We ___ ready tomorrow.
Fala
Diga:

I’m a student.
I was tired yesterday.
I’ll be successful.
Depois fale 3 frases sobre você:

Presente
Passado
Futuro
Leitura
John is a teacher. Last year he was a student. Next year he will be a director.

Responda em inglês:

What is John now?
What was he last year?
What will he be next year?
Escrita
Transforme em negativa (usando contrações):

She is ready.
They were late.
He will be here.
Transforme em pergunta:

She is happy.
They were at home.
He will be ready.
Fala
Fale por 5 frases:

Como você está hoje.
Como estava ontem.
Como estará amanhã.
Use contrações.

Leitura
Anna is a scientist. Five years ago she was not famous. In the future, she will be very successful.

Responda em inglês:

What is Anna?
Was she famous five years ago?
How will she be in the future?
Escrita
Escreva sobre:

Quem você é agora.
Quem você era há dez anos.
Quem você será daqui 10 anos.
